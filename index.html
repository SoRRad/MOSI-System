Methodology and embedded research values

MOSI-S

  • M1 BMI 30.0-34.9; M2 35.0-39.9; M3 40.0-44.9; M4 45.0-49.9; M5 BMI ≥50.0.
  • O score includes hypertension, dysglycemia, obstructive sleep apnea, and hyperlipidemia.
  • GERD is not part of the O score.

Stage

  • Stage IV: Si3, with IV-A and IV-B descriptive subgroup labels.
  • Stage III: Si2, or Si1 with M ≥4 or O ≥3.
  • Stage I: Si0 with M ≤2 and O ≤1. Stage II: all remaining combinations.

MOSI-T

  • Tier A target ≥7.5% TWL.
  • Tier B target ≥15% TWL.
  • Tier C target ≥25% TWL.
  • Tier D target ≥30% TWL.

MOSI-D

  • Shows SG and RYGB research estimates only.
  • Displays thresholds ≥5%, ≥10%, ≥20%, ≥25%, ≥30%, and ≥35% TWL.
  • Unavailable exact values display as N/A; no interpolation is used.

Cohort

  • Staged cohort: n = 3,192.
  • Primary 12-month analytic cohort: n = 2,939.
  • Primary endpoint: TWL ≥20% at 12 months.

Model

  • MOSI + Procedure AUC: 0.761.
  • MOSI + Procedure + Age + Sex AUC: 0.789.
  • MOSI-T distribution: A 248; B 654; C 1,549; D 488.
