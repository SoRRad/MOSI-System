    Methods / details

    M score

    M1: BMI 30.0–34.9; M2: 35.0–39.9; M3: 40.0–44.9; M4: 45.0–49.9; M5: BMI ≥50.0.

    O score

    One point each for hypertension, dysglycemia, obstructive sleep apnea, and hyperlipidemia. GERD is not part of O score.

    Si level

    Si1 is diabetic microvascular/end-organ disease. Si2 is major organ dysfunction. Si3 is dialysis-dependent renal failure or solid-organ transplant recipient status.

    Stage and tier

    • Si3 maps to Stage IV, with IV-A for transplant without dialysis and IV-B for dialysis.
    • Si2 maps to Stage III; Si1 with M ≥4 or O ≥3 maps to Stage III.
    • Therapeutic target tier uses the highest qualifying tier target.

    About the MOSI System

    The MOSI System is a research decision-support calculator for obesity severity staging, therapeutic weight-loss target estimation, and procedure-specific bariatric outcome estimates.

    Developed by the Artificial Intelligence in Surgical Technologies (AIST) Lab, Mayo Clinic.

    Developed as a research platform to support bariatric surgery phenotyping and prospective validation.

    In methodology documentation, MOSI-S refers to severity stage, MOSI-T to therapeutic target, and MOSI-D to decision-support estimates.

    Not a medical device. Not a substitute for clinician judgment. Estimates are derived from retrospective single-center data and should be interpreted in context.
