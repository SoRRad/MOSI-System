    Methods / details

    M score

    M1: BMI 30.0-34.9; M2: 35.0-39.9; M3: 40.0-44.9; M4: 45.0-49.9; M5: BMI >=50.0.

    O score

    One point each for hypertension, dysglycemia, obstructive sleep apnea, and hyperlipidemia. GERD is not part of O score.

    Si level

    Si1 is diabetic microvascular/end-organ disease. Si2 is major organ dysfunction. Si3 is dialysis-dependent renal failure or solid-organ transplant recipient status.

    Stage and tier

    • Si3 maps to Stage IV, with IV-A for transplant without dialysis and IV-B for dialysis.
    • Si2 maps to Stage III; Si1 with M >=4 or O >=3 maps to Stage III.
    • MOSI-T uses the highest qualifying tier target.
    This static calculator does not store or submit entered values.
