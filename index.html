📋 About the MOSI System

patients · single tertiary centre · Mayo Clinic

Pre-publication
Internal validation in progress via this platform
The Metabolic & Obesity Staging Index (MOSI) is a three-dimensional bariatric surgery staging system (M score × O score × Si level) assigning patients to Stages I, II, III, 4A, or 4B. The MOSI-T tier system maps each patient's comorbidity profile to a minimum TWL target for metabolic goal achievement.
Manuscript in preparation. This tool is for clinical audit and research purposes only — it does not constitute a regulated medical device.
