Methodology Notes

Definitions and manuscript-aligned research results used by this static calculator.

MOSI-S Definition

  • M score: M1 BMI 30.0-34.9; M2 35.0-39.9; M3 40.0-44.9; M4 45.0-49.9; M5 BMI ≥50.0.
  • O score adds one point each for hypertension, dysglycemia, obstructive sleep apnea, and hyperlipidemia. GERD is not part of O score.
  • Si0 has no end-organ involvement. Si1 includes diabetic microvascular or end-organ disease. Si2 includes major organ dysfunction. Si3 includes dialysis-dependent renal failure or solid-organ transplant recipient status.
  • Stage IV is assigned for Si3. Stage III is assigned for Si2, or for Si1 with M ≥4 or O ≥3. Stage I requires Si0 with M ≤2 and O ≤1. All remaining combinations are Stage II.

MOSI-T Definition

  • Tier A: target ≥7.5% TWL when no qualifying metabolic or organ comorbidity is present.
  • Tier B: target ≥15% TWL for obstructive sleep apnea, hypertension, hyperlipidemia, GERD, arthropathy, or prior arthroplasty.
  • Tier C: target ≥25% TWL for type 2 diabetes, impaired fasting glucose, or diabetic microvascular/end-organ disease.
  • Tier D: target ≥30% TWL for cardiac history, stroke/TIA, Si2, or Si3 systemic disease. Highest tier wins.

MOSI-D Display

  • MOSI-D displays decision-support estimates for sleeve gastrectomy (SG) and Roux-en-Y gastric bypass (RYGB).
  • Thresholds shown: ≥5%, ≥10%, ≥20%, ≥25%, ≥30%, and ≥35% TWL at 12 and 60 months.
  • Only available documented values are displayed. Missing threshold cells remain N/A.
  • Procedure-specific probability estimates may support discussion and require bariatric surgeon judgment.

Final Study Numbers

  • Staged cohort: n = 3,192.
  • Primary 12-month analytic cohort: n = 2,939.
  • Primary endpoint: TWL ≥20% at 12 months.
  • MOSI-T distribution: Tier A 248; Tier B 654; Tier C 1,549; Tier D 488.
