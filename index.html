📋 About the MOSI System
Derivation Cohort
3,097
patients · single tertiary centre · Mayo Clinic
Validation Phase
Pre-publication
Internal validation in progress via this platform
The Metabolic & Obesity Staging Index (MOSI) is a three-dimensional bariatric surgery staging system (M score × O score × Si level) that assigns patients to Stages I, II, III, 4A, or 4B. The MOSI-T tier system maps each patient's comorbidity profile to a minimum TWL target required for metabolic goal achievement. This platform simultaneously provides real-time clinical decision support and prospectively captures surgeon feedback to constitute the internal validation dataset.
Manuscript in preparation. Citation will be added upon publication. This tool is intended for clinical audit and research purposes — it does not constitute a regulated medical device.
